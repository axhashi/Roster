About

Third-time founder working on the problem I kept hitting as an operator: hiring runs on unverifiable claims. Candidates inflate, employers ghost, and nobody carries a portable reputation across the process. SkillFleet is a two-sided AI hiring marketplace with a conduct-grading trust engine — employers are graded on how they actually treat candidates, and candidate skills are verified rather than self-reported. Previously built and exited in DTC and marketplace. Interested in: marketplace liquidity, trust systems, verification infrastructure, and anything that makes hiring less adversarial.
